## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainer status is granted by the TSC or by existing repository maintainers based on a track record of sustained, quality contributions. To be considered, a contributor should demonstrate:

- Consistent contributions over time (code, reviews, documentation)
- Understanding of project goals and architecture
- Constructive participation in community discussions
- Adherence to the project's Code of Conduct and Contributing guidelines
